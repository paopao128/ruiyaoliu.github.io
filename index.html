Ruiyao Liu

Ruiyao Liu is currently a Phd student in The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of north texas. He is a member of Mobile Computing Lab (MCLab) at UNT. Ruiyao Liu received his B.Engr. degree in Automation from Sichuan University in China in 2023.

The focus of Ruiyao Liu's research is privacy.
